1. Who are we ?

The Humanitarian's Dream, commonly known as THD, is a non-profit humanitarian organisation dedicated to improving and promoting the status of children. We are an agency that enables you to make humanitarian trips. THD was founded on 8 October 2004. THD's priorities are: girls' education, immunisation and AIDS, child protection, human health and gender equality.
